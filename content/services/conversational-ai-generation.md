# Conversational AI Generation (CAG)

Development of sophisticated conversational AI systems that provide natural, context-aware interactions—including coding and operations agents that act with tools under clear boundaries. Services include:

- Conversational AI strategy and architecture design
- Custom AI assistant development with domain-specific knowledge
- Multi-agent conversation and task-orchestration systems
- Agentic coding and operator assistants (plan → act → verify loops)
- Persona design and conversation flow optimization
- Natural language processing pipeline development
- Intent recognition and entity extraction systems
- Integration with business systems, repos, and knowledge bases
- Continuous learning and improvement frameworks
- Chatbot analytics and performance monitoring
- Voice-enabled conversational interfaces
- Enterprise knowledge base integration
- Multi-channel conversational AI deployment
- Customer service automation solutions
- Regulatory compliance for conversational systems
