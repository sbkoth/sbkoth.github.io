# Observability Engineering

Looking beyond simple monitoring, our expertise transforms complex system data into actionable intelligence. Through our advanced observability implementations, your team gains the ability to predict issues before they affect users and drastically reduce mean time to resolution. Our comprehensive approach covers:

- End-to-end observability architecture design
- Prometheus and Grafana implementation for metrics collection and visualization
- Distributed tracing with Jaeger, Zipkin, and OpenTelemetry
- Log aggregation and analysis with ELK Stack and Loki
- Enterprise monitoring with Dynatrace, Datadog, and Splunk
- Application instrumentation with Micrometer and other metrics libraries
- Financial transaction monitoring and alerting
- Health check design for application liveness and readiness
- SLO/SLI definition and implementation
- Actionable alerting and incident response workflows
- Custom dashboards for business and technical KPIs
- Performance bottleneck identification and resolution
- Cost optimization for observability infrastructure
- Correlation of metrics, logs, and traces
- Capacity planning using historical telemetry data
