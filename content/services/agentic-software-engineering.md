# Agentic Software Engineering

Help teams adopt agentic coding as a durable engineering practice—not a one-off demo. Design workflows where coding agents implement against clear specs while humans own product intent, architecture, security, and release criteria.

## What we deliver

- Agentic workflow design for product and platform teams
- Spec → plan → implement → verify pipelines tailored to your stack
- Tooling and guardrails (repo conventions, test gates, capability modes)
- Multi-agent orchestration patterns for research, coding, and review
- Integration of coding agents with CI, static analysis, and deploy pipelines
- Dead-code audits, import-graph hygiene, and bundle discipline under agent load
- Terminal-first and developer-experience surfaces that agents and humans share
- Training for engineers on effective prompting, review, and “evidence before done”
- Risk controls: secrets handling, scope boundaries, and production safety checklists
- Office hours / embedded pairing to stand up agentic practice on a real codebase
