# Program & Project Management

Bringing decades of collective experience managing complex enterprise initiatives across financial services and technology sectors. Excellence in both strategic program leadership and tactical project execution, ensuring critical business initiatives are delivered on time, within budget, and with measurable business impact.

- **Financial Management** - Oversight of program budgets exceeding $10M with detailed forecasting, variance analysis, and financial planning
- **Resource Optimization** - Allocation of resources strategically across program workstreams to maximize delivery efficiency and manage dependencies
- **Stakeholder Management** - Facilitation of executive steering committees, management of cross-functional stakeholder requirements, and driving organizational alignment
- **Benefit Realization** - Implementation of KPI tracking frameworks to measure program outcomes and ensure benefit delivery against strategic objectives
